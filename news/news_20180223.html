--- layout: news-release title: CGCA - News 2018-02-23 description: News release 2018-02-23 heading: Prof. Dawn Erb Named Kavli Frontiers of Science Fellow lead: UWM Physics professor Dawn Erb was named a Kavli Frontiers of Science Fellow by the National Academy of Sciences date: February 23, 2018 ---

UWM Physics professor Dawn Erb was named a Kavli Frontiers of Science Fellow by the National Academy of Sciences. According to their website:

The Kavli Frontiers of Science symposium series (KFoS) is the Academy's premiere activity for distinguished young scientists. Attendance at a KFoS symposium is by invitation only and attendees are selected from among award winners for early career scientists in the US and abroad.
